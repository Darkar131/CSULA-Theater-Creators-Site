Welcome to House of Oeuvre Website

This website belongs to House of Oeuvre Performing Arts Club, a student-runned organization in California State University of Los Angeles. We are a club that is dedicated to generating more connections between the Cal State LA students and promoting collaborations and creations from the Cal State LA art community. We welcome all CSLA artists and students to use this website to find more about us and our upcoming events and projects. If you want to use Cal State LA Creators, our second website that is a casting call page for all CSLA students, please click the this link to the other website in Contact Us

Frequent Ask Questions

Why did we create Cal State LA Creators?

We noticed that there is a huge gap between all the arts major and we want to bridge that gap. There has been students from different majors who needs students from another major for their project. The only way of us to advertise our projects has been posting out small flyers around the campus, but it lacks effectivity and accessibility. This website will help all art majors work and collaborate together in a quick and easy way.

How is this website more effective than any other way to promote jobs for artists?

You see flyers all around campus advertising jobs for artists and maybe an occasional email, but very rarely will those calls be answered. Our website is the most effective way to let artists know of any job opportunities because we have so many different jobs in one place. You are able to look up any job posting you want or create your own job posting with your own computer. It's accessible 24/7

How soon will my job posting be available on the site?

We check submissions daily so that people can post their jobs as soon as possible. Once the date in which your audition or casting call has passed, the job posting will be deleted the day after to create room for more job postings.
